Tips for Tony 💡

🎯 About Losing Belly Fat

  • You cannot spot-reduce belly fat with crunches — but you can burn it with consistent strength training + cardio
  • Building muscle raises your resting metabolism — so you burn more calories even at rest
  • Visceral fat (the deep belly kind) is actually the first to go when you train consistently — great news!
  • The cardio finishers at the end of each workout are specifically designed to torch belly fat
  • Aim for 8 glasses of water a day — hydration accelerates fat metabolism

🦵 Protecting Your Knee

  • On the Leg Press, never go past 90° — a partial range of motion is completely fine and much safer
  • Avoid any deep squatting movements — the machines in this plan are specifically chosen to avoid those
  • If the knee feels warm or swollen after a session, ice it for 15 minutes and skip leg work next time
  • The bike warm-up is chosen over the treadmill on lower body days — easier on the joint
  • Strong glutes and hamstrings actually protect your knee — these exercises build that support system

⚙️ Getting the Most Out of Planet Fitness

  • Ask a staff member to show you the machine layout on your first visit — they're very helpful
  • The 30-minute express circuit is NOT what we're doing — you're on the full floor
  • Go at off-peak times (mid-morning weekdays) to have machines to yourself
  • Breathe out on the effort, in on the return — never hold your breath
  • Move with slow, controlled form — no jerking or swinging

🍽️ Food Makes the Biggest Difference

  • You can't out-train a bad diet — but you don't need to be extreme
  • Eat protein at every meal (eggs, chicken, fish, Greek yogurt) — it preserves muscle while losing fat
  • Eat lighter dinners — visceral fat accumulation is strongly linked to evening eating
  • Cut back on white bread, pasta, and sugary drinks — these feed belly fat directly
  • A protein snack within 1 hour after training (eggs, cottage cheese) speeds up recovery

🏆 The Big Picture

At 81 and already fit, Tony has a massive advantage — his body knows how to work hard. Consistency over 8–12 weeks is where the real changes happen. The belly will shrink, strength will build, and energy levels will improve. Every session counts. Show up, do the work, and trust the process. 💙
